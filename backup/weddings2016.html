Denham Grove

Our lovely bride Rosanna married her love Sam at an elegant wedding in Denham Grove. The pair chose a rich wine colour theme which could be seen throughout the wedding, including the bridesmaids’ dresses. For the bride, we complemented a white avalanche rose bouquet with stunning amnesia roses to give a stylish, aesthetic look, adding red eucalyptus and sedum to the bridesmaids bouquets for that extra splash of colour. The white avalanche rose theme continued for the table displayed, drawing in early grey and off white to add a vintage touch.

Commercial Road, East London

This quirky boho London wedding featured large chunky tables and eccentric props which made for a bold backdrop to the fabulous venue of 4th Floor Studio, Commercial Road, East London.  Hannah was such a lovely bride to work with, and her easy going personality shone out with her choice of jam jars, and bottles filled with the brightest wild flowers, which had been her wish after Mark proposed to her in London Fields Park, Hackney in amongst the wild flowers. We filled the room with jars of flowers everywhere for a truly stunning effect.

Helmingham Hall, Suffolk

This celebrity wedding took place in the exquisite Helmingham Hall, Suffolk and featured a beautiful array of white avalanche and amnesia roses. The arch consisted of a total of 400 white roses, perfectly creating the bride's fairytale vision. The table display showcased a mix of roses and large white hydrangea - a beautiful and versatile flower.
