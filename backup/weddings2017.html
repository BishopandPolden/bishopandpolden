The Priory Barn, Little Wymondley

Katie and Umberto married in a wonderful venue set in the countryside of Little Wymondley - a fairytale venue for a magical day. The flower theme was peaches and cream, which perfectly complimented the bridesmaids' minty dresses. Tall conical vases were topped with large floral displays of hydrangea, peonies and roses, and floral displays hung on each post around the barn with large podiums of flowers at each end of the top table. The venue was beautifully lit with candle light to really set the scene.
