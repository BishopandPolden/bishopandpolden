Bishop and Polden Floral Events is a young and vibrant company based in London. We specialise in wedding flowers, from small intimate venues to large and extravagant marquees. As event florists we also cover corporate gatherings, private dinner parties and family celebrations.

After completing an advanced classic flower design diploma with Greenwich University, Bishop and Polden Floral Events was born.

Founders Anna Bishop and Caroline Polden have a passion for flowers and floral design. We create beautiful and individual designs for weddings of distinction. We are organised, creative, meticulous and offer a personal service with attention to every detail, from initial consultation right up to the big day.

With a small team of experienced florists to call upon, we provide flowers for parties, corporate events and private dinners, and hire out equipment such as vases, pedestals and candelabras.
